The Challenge

The client was evaluating a capital-intensive investment in the energy sector — a Central Gas Station in Msayleh, South Lebanon. The decision required clear visibility on financial viability, operational structure, market positioning, and long-term sustainability within a complex and competitive market.

Without a rigorous feasibility framework, the risk of misallocating capital or underestimating operational costs was significant. The client needed structured financial intelligence to move from concept to confident investment decision.

Scope of Advisory Work
  • Full Feasibility Study — Comprehensive analysis covering market viability, demand assessment, and competitive positioning
  • Financial Modeling — Multi-scenario financial projections, revenue forecasting, and sensitivity analysis
  • CAPEX / OPEX Structuring — Detailed capital expenditure planning and operational cost architecture
  • Risk Identification & Scenario Analysis — Stress-testing the investment under varying market and cost conditions
  • Strategic Investment Advisory — Positioning the project for sustainable long-term performance
Impact Delivered
✦ Results
  • Enabled confident investment decision-making
  • Provided structured financial clarity and visibility
  • Defined a sustainable operational framework
  • Supported transition from concept to execution
  • Identified key risk factors before capital commitment
  • Project cleared for implementation — Q2 2026
