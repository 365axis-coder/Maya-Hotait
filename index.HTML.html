About

Transformation is not an intervention.
It is structural design.

With over two decades of executive and advisory experience, Maya Hotait works at the intersection of governance, risk architecture, and financial sustainability — supporting organizations navigating complexity, restructuring, or strategic growth across MENA, Africa, and Eurasia.

She partners directly with boards, executives, and institutional leadership to strengthen decision frameworks, redesign operating models, and align financial strategy with long-term institutional resilience.

Because credibility is not claimed.
